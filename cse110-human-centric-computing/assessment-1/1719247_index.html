About

I grew up in the state of Michigan and moved to China in 2017. Before moving to China, I lived in Aachen, Germany for six years. I've had an interest in computer science and technology from a young age and began a formal education in software development at AixpertSoft GmbH and BWV Aachen in 2013. After I completed my Ausbildung, I worked for a consulting company as a junior specialist implementing ServiceNow ERP solutions.

I currently live in Suzhou and attend XJTLU, a Sino-British partnership university between Xi'an Jiatong University and the University of Liverpool. My interests include expanding and improving upon my software development skills, traveling, film and music, language and culture, trying new food, and meeting new people. Luckily, moving to Suzhou and joining XJTLU has allowed me to pursue all of those interests!
